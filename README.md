# Fenadis

**fenadis** it is a project focused on liberating a planet long oppressed by the decisions of a conquering federation. Decades ago, this federation imposed its will on the planet and its creatures, leaving a legacy of control and forgotten potential. Now is the time to challenge those old decisions and restore freedom and hope to this world.

# The mod adds:

A new planet that brings with it exclusively 156 blocks, 23 sectors with a difficulty that increases as you advance in the hostile world and as a personal addiction the story of the tragedy that happened to the celestial body Fenadis and how it came to such a state today.

# Highlight some points

- The creation of units is similar to Erekir, with the difference that each assembler offers 5 units of the same tier and there are modules linked only for their construction.
- The transportation of resources is mainly carried out in batches, for example on stacked conveyors.
- Attack sectors abound in this mod.
- In the "logic" section there is nothing like that and I have used it to introduce "special" category structures.
